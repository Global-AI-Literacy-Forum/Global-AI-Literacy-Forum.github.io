Welcome!

The Global AI Literacy Forum is a non-profit forging pathways to Artificial Intelligence understanding for everyone, everywhere.

We aspite to tackle the digital divide, ensuring that the advancements in AI benefit all corners of society, from classrooms to communities, fostering innovation and inclusivity.
