Laying the Foundation

While our journey has just begun, we are actively designing projects to make a tangible differences in line with our mission. The initiatives outlined below represent our initial focus areas as we grow and build momentum.

Digital Access Hub

Pilot Initiative: Digital Library in Nepal

A significant barrier to digital literacy is the lack of fundamental access to resources and technology, albeit due to various reasons.

Our pilot project aims to launch a hub in a rural Nepal, providing infrastructure. We hope this initiative helps in mitigate the divide arised due to lack of access.

Workshops & Training

Insights into AI Principles & Ethics

Understanding AI goes beyond just using the tools; it involves grasping the core concepts, ethical considerations, and societal implications. We plan to create a workshops by partnering with experts and institutions to spread the 'AI Knowledge'.
