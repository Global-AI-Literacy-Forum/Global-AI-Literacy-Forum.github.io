Mission Statement

Building an AI Literate Community, Bridging the Digital Divide

Global AI Literacy Forum, Inc. is a dedicated Not-for-Profit Organization committed to enhancing Artificial Intelligence (AI) Literacy, focusing on South Asia and the US.

We cover a wide range of domains related to infrastructure, education, tools, and emerging technologies. Our core goal is to build an adequately informed and digital-literate community, actively work to bridge the digital gap, and enable individuals and society to harness cutting-edge technology for collective benefit.

We believe that understanding and access to AI should not be limited to tech experts, but should be available to everyone, fostering a future where technology serves humanity equitably.

Challanges Identified

What challages have we identified?

Digital Divide

Millions still lack access to basic digital technology. Furthermore, many who do have such access are not well equipped with the adequate digital literacy to effectively utilize these tools, including AI, actively hindering their ability to participate fully in this dynamic world.

Rapid Rise of AI

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly shaping our habits, lives and even society. To ensure such consequential technology is used ethically and beneficially ("for good"), broad public understanding of its working is crucial. This need extends beyond the tech sector to include students, farmers, educators, artists, and citizens in every field.

Inclusive Education

Formal education systems, while essential, are often slow to adapt. In a field where progress is overnight and progress feels like magic, there's an urgent need for accessible, practical, and relevant programs to increase AI awareness and informal programs tailored for diverse audiences, ensuring everyone can learn, adapt, and thrive.
